Since I was around 8 years old, I have had a real fond place in my heart for collecting, playing & painting minature figurines from a table top war game called Warhammer 40,000.
The game requires players to have a certain amount of models, formed into groups called units, which then makes up an army that players use to play against other players on a battlefield of set proportions. The battlefield size is stated in the games rules.
